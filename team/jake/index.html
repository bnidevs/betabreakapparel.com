Jake
The Frog
@jakeangclimbsrocks

Hyla japonica, also known as the Japanese tree frog, found in several countries across East Asia, has suctioning toes that let it stick to surfaces, and a potential jump distance of approximately 20 times its body length. The only thing Jake is missing is the relatively long tongue. In his habitat, he is usually found in midair, landing on holds others would consider heinous, and catching himself with minimal difficulty.
