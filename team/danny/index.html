Danny
The Artist
@_amateur.climber_

Slow, smooth strokes make up the paintings that Danny composes with his hands and feet while he’s on the wall. You have to have a bit of extra patience to witness the send come alive. But rest assured, the museums will come fighting to put up his work, and people will line up to see it. His style is iconic, you don’t need to see his signature to know it’s him. The critics have only one gripe: “If only he was more prolific!”
