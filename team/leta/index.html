Leta
The Nomad
@letadickinson

If you try to look for Leta where you last saw them, there might only be chalk dust and cobwebs left. We really never know where in the world they might be, and we are even less likely to know what they’re climbing. Having an accurate mailing address on their driver’s license is at the very bottom of their priorities. As a Bishop highball conqueror, there’s really not much rock Leta’s afraid to touch. And being the wanderlust she is, there’s even less she can’t touch.
