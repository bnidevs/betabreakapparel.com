The Doctor
@bigzee.png

“She’ll fix you right up.” We imagine saying to our rather roughed up drafts of designs that eventually make it onto the backs of climbers who soldier up solid rock. But not before they’ve paid a visit to Big Zee. As a fundamental step in most of our art pipeline, Zara will take concepts and sketches that are beat up, discolored, bleeding, unbalanced, and otherwise traumatized, and make them whole. Step into her office, no salt on wounds here, only magic.
